what's a deliverable?

a deliverable is one user-facing outcome. a landing page, an email, a checkout step, a modal flow. if a user would experience it as "one thing," it's one deliverable. a 4-step signup wizard? that's 4 deliverables, shipped one after another.

how does it work?

you get a shared trello board. drop requests in using a simple template, i scope them, build them, and mark them done. you'll always know what's in progress and what's next. everything's async by default. no standing meetings, no status calls, just work shipping.

what's the difference between the tiers?

build retainer is execution. you know exactly what you want, i build it. no strategy, no calls, just output.

growth partner is strategic. i help you figure out what to build, then build it. you get weekly updates, quarterly roadmaps, and someone thinking about your growth, not just completing tasks.

can i switch between tiers?

yes. flex down to build retainer during quieter months. flex back up to growth partner when you need more strategic input, subject to availability.
